Why we built this

Three quiet beliefs the whole product is built on.

Klasly is shaped by feedback from real studio owners running real classes — what they actually need, and what they don't. The principles below decide every feature we ship, every line of pricing, every screen we say no to.

01

Teachers should teach.

Set Klasly up once and forget it's there. Sessions auto-generate four weeks ahead. Waitlists promote themselves. Reminders go out without you. Most days, you shouldn't need to open the app at all.

02

Money goes where the work is.

$190 a year, not $1,548. The fewer dollars going to your software vendor, the more left for the people teaching, the space you rent, the community you're building. And no end-of-month payroll: Stripe Connect splits every payment as it happens, so instructors get their share the moment a member books.

03

Less, on purpose.

We say no to features that don't serve a small studio. Every checkbox you don't have to tick, every screen you don't have to learn, is one less thing standing between you and the work.

How Klasly works

Four jobs, one console, zero apps to glue together.

From morning class to retreat deposit to month-end payout, the same data flows everywhere. No reconciling four tools at 11 pm.

01 / SCHEDULE

A class calendar that holds everything.

Set classes once; Klasly auto-generates four weeks of sessions ahead. Members book, waitlists fill on cancellations, instructors swap with auto-notify. No double-booking, no Sunday-night spreadsheets.

02 / RETREATS

Retreat pages that handle even the deposit.

Photo gallery, daily schedule, map, packing list. Three-installment payments charged on schedule with reminders the day before each charge. Group bookings, waitlist that promotes itself.

03 / COLLECTIVE

Payouts that go straight to the teachers.

Stripe Connect routes member payments directly to each instructor's account. Your studio fee is deducted automatically. Hour-tier memberships bill overage at month-end. Zero manual transfers.

04 / COMMUNICATION

One thread per member. Forever.

In-app messages, studio-wide announcements, community board. Web push for one-hour-before reminders. Email campaigns to lists. Members reply in the app; you see every conversation in context.

FAQ

Questions studio owners ask.

If yours isn't here, write to support@klasly.app. We read everything.

Is the trial really free? Do I need a card?

Yes. Thirty days, no card upfront. You only enter payment details if you decide to keep using Klasly past day 30.

Can I bring my members and schedule across?

Yes. Members import via CSV; recurring classes are recreated in minutes. Klasly auto-generates four weeks of sessions, so you set each class up only once.

How does retreat booking actually work?

Create a retreat and Klasly assembles a public page with gallery, daily schedule, map, packing list, early-bird pricing, waitlist, group booking, and three-installment payments. You share one link — everything happens there.

For shared studios — can instructors collect their own payments?

Yes. That's collective mode. Each instructor connects their own Stripe account. Member payments go straight to them; your studio fee is deducted automatically. No middleman, no monthly transfers.

What's the 0.5% platform fee?

A flat 0.5% on member payments processed through Klasly, separate from Stripe's processing fee. That's how $190 a year covers every feature with no tiers — and how we keep the bill small enough that the savings go back to your teachers.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Klasly is a PWA — members and instructors install it like a native app, and it works offline for viewing schedules and member info.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, from your dashboard. Annual plans are refunded pro-rata for unused months.
