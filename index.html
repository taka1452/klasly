Core Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Stop paying for 100 features when you use 6. Klasly gives you exactly what a small studio needs — beautifully simple.

Class Scheduling & Booking

Set up your classes once. Klasly auto-generates sessions for weeks ahead. Members book online 24/7. Waitlist fills spots automatically when someone cancels.

Payments & Billing

Stripe-powered automatic billing. Monthly subscriptions, class packs, and drop-ins. See every payment. Get alerted on failures. No spreadsheets needed.

Member Management

All your members in one place — plans, credits, status, visit history. Import existing data from CSV. Manage everything from a single dashboard.

Digital Waivers

Send signature requests by email. Members sign online. Track who's signed and who hasn't. Minor waivers go directly to parents — no account needed.

In-App Messaging

Message your members directly inside the app. Unread badges, read receipts, and email notifications. No more switching between apps to communicate.

Events & Retreats

Multi-day events with room options, 3-installment payment plans, custom cancellation policies, and application forms. Guests can book without an account.

Shared Studio Mode

Built for studios where instructors rent space and run their own business.

If your studio works like an Airbnb for wellness — instructors pay rent, set their own prices, and collect their own payments — no other software was built for you. Until now.

Self-Scheduling

Instructors book rooms and add classes themselves. No more back-and-forth. Double-booking is prevented automatically.

Direct Payments + Studio Fee

Student payments go directly to the instructor's Stripe. Your studio fee percentage is automatically deducted. Zero manual transfers.

Tier Memberships & Hour Tracking

Set up rent tiers with monthly hours. Usage is tracked in 15-minute increments. Overage fees are billed automatically.

Studio Pass & Revenue Split

Sell a monthly pass for all classes. At month-end, revenue is automatically split among instructors based on participation.
