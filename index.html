Why we built this

A tool should make your life
smaller, not bigger.

I ran a small yoga studio — 34 members, three instructors, one shared Google Sheet that everyone was terrified to touch.

I was paying $147/month for studio software. It had a built-in marketing suite, an e-commerce store, an event ticketing module, and an analytics dashboard I opened exactly once. I didn't need any of it. I needed to know who was coming to the 7am class.

One Thursday morning, a member asked me at the door if her 10-class pass had expired. I didn't know. I told her to wait a second, went to the back room, opened my laptop, navigated through four screens, and found the answer. She'd used 9 classes. She bought a new pass. The whole thing took four minutes.

Four minutes. For one answer. That one studio owner should know by heart.

That afternoon I sat down and wrote the first lines of what became Klasly. Not because I'm a developer — I'm not — but because I was embarrassed. My software was making me worse at my job.

The goal was simple: build something so obvious, so quiet, so out of the way that you forget it's running. One screen to see today's classes. One screen to see your members. Payments that just work. That's it.

What is the 0.5%?

When a member pays for a membership or class pack through Klasly, we take a 0.5% platform fee in addition to Stripe's standard card processing fees. You keep the rest — 99.5% goes to you. On a $2,000/month studio, that's just $10 extra.

Powered by Stripe

All payments are processed by Stripe — the same platform trusted by Amazon and Shopify. Stripe's standard card fees (2.9% + 30¢) apply separately and go directly to Stripe, not us.
